Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

Overview

SPC Tools is designed to work primarily on-device. We do not collect or sell your personal data. Your calculation history is stored locally on your device unless you choose to remove it.

Data We Collect

  • On-device calculation history and preferences (stored locally on your device).
  • Subscription and purchase information processed by our payment provider.

How We Use Data

  • To provide app functionality such as calculations and history.
  • To verify subscription status and unlock premium features.

Third-Party Services

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect purchase and device information necessary to validate subscriptions. Their processing is governed by their own privacy policy.

Data Retention

Your calculation history remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.

Your Choices

  • You can delete individual history items or clear all history in the app.
  • You can stop using the app at any time by uninstalling it.
