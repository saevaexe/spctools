# Screenshot Data + Steps

Use these inputs so screenshots look consistent and show clear results.

## 1) Onboarding
- Launch app fresh (delete app or reset `hasSeenOnboarding` in Settings > Storage).
- Capture Welcome page.

## 2) Home (Categories Grid)
- Open app normally.
- Ensure search is empty.
- If Pro banner is visible, keep it on screen.

## 3) Calculator (Cp/Cpk inputs)
Open Cp/Cpk and enter:
- USL: 12
- LSL: 4
- Mean (μ): 8
- Sigma (σ): 1

## 4) Results (Cp/Cpk)
- Tap Calculate.
- Expected: Cp and Cpk around 1.33 and interpretation "capable".
- Capture results + interpretation.
- Tap Save History once (for History screenshot).

## 5) Control Chart
Open Control Chart and keep chart type = I-MR.
Data input (paste as space-separated values):
10 10.5 9.8 10.2 10.1 10.3 10.0 20.0 10.2 10.1 9.9
- Tap Calculate.
- Out-of-control warning should appear (1 point).
- Tap Save History once.

## 6) History
Open History tab. You should see at least:
- Cp/Cpk
- Control Chart

## 7) Paywall
From Home, tap any Pro category card (or the trial/expired banner).
- Capture the paywall screen.

## 8) Settings
Open Settings tab.
- Capture Restore Purchases + Version.
